    About This Research

    The Digital Calorie Project applies nutritional science concepts to digital consumption. Just as we track food calories to maintain physical health, tracking "digital calories" helps us understand and manage our relationship with technology.

    Our research examines how different types of digital content affect mental wellbeing, productivity, and social connections. By quantifying screen time usage, we aim to promote healthier digital habits.
